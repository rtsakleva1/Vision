LOCATION

The location of the festival area is 8 km after the village of Zheleznitsa on the road to the village of Plana.
It can be reached in any way: by bus, car, taxi, hitchhiking, bike or even on foot along the trails and wooded labyrinths of Vitosha.
From Sofia Public transport you can use busses 70,69 and 98. For those who prefer to come with their own car they should take a turn after the Popova Mahala stop (or the Plana sign in general) turns right at the fork, where there is free parking and entrance to the festival by the river.For those who prefer to come with their own four-wheeled vehicle, a turn after the Popova Mahala stop (or the Plana sign in general) turns right at the fork, where there is free parking and entrance to the festival by the river.
The distance to the festival area from the center of Sofia is about 35 km.
